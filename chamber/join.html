NP Membership Benefits

This membership is exclusively for non-profit organizations and comes with no annual fee. Benefits include:

  • Basic listing in the Business Directory.
  • Access to community events.
  • Monthly newsletter updates.
  • Opportunities for local networking.
×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze tier offers fundamental support for small businesses. Benefits include:

  • Enhanced listing in the Business Directory.
  • Invitations to exclusive Bronze-level networking events.
  • Discounted rates on chamber workshops and training sessions.
  • Access to basic business resources.
×

Silver Membership Benefits

Designed for growing businesses, the Silver membership provides significant advantages. Benefits include:

  • Prominent listing in the Business Directory with logo inclusion.
  • Priority invitations to all networking events.
  • Free access to select business development workshops.
  • Opportunities for local advertising in chamber publications.
  • Eligibility for "Member Spotlight" features on the home page (random selection).
×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium Gold tier offers comprehensive benefits for established businesses seeking maximum visibility and support. Benefits include:

  • Top-tier, featured listing in the Business Directory with premium placement.
  • VIP access to all chamber events and exclusive leadership forums.
  • Complimentary access to all business development workshops and seminars.
  • Dedicated support for business advocacy and government liaison.
  • Guaranteed frequent "Member Spotlight" features on the home page.
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities for major chamber events.
